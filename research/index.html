Research

AI and statistical learning for biodiversity data

My research develops computational methods for biodiversity monitoring, with emphasis on environmental DNA, deep learning, species distribution models and predictive ecology.

Predictive Genomic Ecology
Postdoctoral research · TIMC-IMAG, Université Grenoble Alpes · 2025–2026

Development of statistical and computational methods to predict ecological community responses to environmental change. The project integrates genomic, ecological, environmental and functional data, with a particular focus on species distribution models and biodiversity forecasting.

Predictive ecology Species distribution models Genomic ecology
Machine learning and deep learning for eDNA-based biodiversity monitoring
PhD research · CEFE, ETH Zurich, TIMC-IMAG · 2021–2024

Development of machine learning and deep learning approaches for marine biodiversity monitoring using environmental DNA metabarcoding. The project explored how neural networks can learn ecological information from raw eDNA sequences and biodiversity matrices, with applications to marine protected areas, ecological gradients and large-scale biodiversity assessment.

Environmental DNA Deep learning Marine biodiversity
Computer vision for phytoplankton classification
Research internship · IOCAG, Gran Canaria · Jan–Jun 2021

Development of computer vision methods for the automatic classification of phytoplankton using deep learning algorithms, in the context of climate change research and Atlantic Ocean biodiversity monitoring.
